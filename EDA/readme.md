
# 📊 Step 5:Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 🎯 Objective
To explore cleaned datasets, identify distributions, detect anomalies, and uncover business insights across customers, orders, payments, reviews, products, sellers, and geolocation.

---

## 🔍 Key Analyses

### Orders & Order Items
- Checked order status distribution and delivery delays.  
- Identified anomalies in freight values and unusually long delivery times.  

### Payments
- Analyzed payment methods (credit card, boleto, installments).  
- Found high frequency of installment payments in electronics categories.  

### Reviews
- Explored review score distribution.  
- Linked low scores to delayed deliveries and high freight costs.  

### Products
- Examined product categories and price ranges.  
- Flagged missing category names and extreme price outliers.  

### Sellers
- Validated seller zip code prefixes.  
- Detected unrealistic zip codes and duplicate seller entries.  

### Geolocation
- Mapped customer and seller distribution.  
- Checked latitude/longitude ranges for anomalies.  

### Customers
- Analyzed customer distribution by region.  
- Identified repeat customers and churn patterns.

---

## 📈 Insights
- Installment payments dominate in high‑value product categories.  
- Delivery delays strongly correlate with negative reviews.  
- Freight cost anomalies highlight potential logistics inefficiencies.  
- Seller data requires validation for zip codes and duplicates.  
- Geographic mapping shows concentration of customers in urban regions, sellers more dispersed.
